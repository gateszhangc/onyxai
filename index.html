FAQ

What this first version says clearly.

What is OnyxAI?

OnyxAI is an AI memory assistant built to capture conversations, compress them into useful structure, and make recall feel immediate.

Who is it for?

It is positioned for founders, creators, consultants, and knowledge workers who often think in conversation before they think in polished documents.

Is the waitlist live right now?

Not yet. This first release ships the landing page and a truthful placeholder experience before a real submission backend is connected.

Does this page promise a downloadable app today?

No. The current site introduces the product direction and collects interest without pretending that app distribution is already available.
